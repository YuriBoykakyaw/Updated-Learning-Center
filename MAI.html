AI Engineer Course Overview

This is an Artificial Intelligence Engineer master’s course that is a comprehensive learning approach for mastering the domains of Artificial Intelligence, Data Science, Business Analytics, Business Intelligence, Python coding and Deep Learning with TensorFlow. Upon the completion of the training, you will be able to take on challenging roles in the Artificial Intelligence domain.

What will you learn in this master's program in Artificial Intelligence?
  • Python for Data Science
  • Introduction to Artificial Intelligence domain
  • How Data science and Artificial Intelligence overlap
  • Importance of Python coding for data analytics
  • Efficient design of Machine Learning systems
  • Working with Tableau interactive dashboard and reports
  • MS Excel calculations, tables and formulae
  • R Statistical computing for Data Science
  • Building of Artificial Neural Networks and Statistical Models
  • Deep Learning techniques and working with TensorFlow
